# How to Contribute to the Coinbase Design System

Want to contribute to CDS? Whether you want to add a new component or tweak an existing one, we'll guide you through the whole process from ideation to release.

Before you begin, make sure you've completed your CDS Engineering Onboarding by setting up your environment and familiarizing yourself with our [component conventions](./docs/conventions/overview.md).
