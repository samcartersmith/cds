Cards are containers that showcase content and actions related to a singular topic. By design, Cards organize, highlight, and bundle information. They encourage people to focus on a singular topic and take action if needed. Keep Cards concise, focused, and engaging.

## Actions

The Card itself is typically the primary action. However, we recommend placing a Button in the Footer to encourage engagement or to make the Card feel more actionable. Buttons in the Footer should callout the main CTA of the Card.

<DocImage format="png" component="card" name="actions" />

Cards can also have supplementary actions in the Header or Footer such as:

- **More actions**: This triggers a Select Menu. Use a More icon.
- **Dismissible**: This closes the Card. Use a Close icon.

## Dividers

Generally speaking dividers are used to draw the basic layout of the page and separate distinct modules and sections from each other. Full-width dividers are placed on the top and/or bottom of Card and used to communicate card boundaries. Toggle on top/bottom dividers when grouping or stacking cards together.

Related component: [Dividers](/components/divider)

## Sizing & Scale

Cards scale and adapt to different screen sizes. Although you could apply a fixed-width to a Card, we recommend determining Card widths based on the Grid. Card height is dependent on the grid and type of content within the card. 
Cards that are set in a tile grid should all be the same height.
