## Behavior

<br />

### Opening and closing

<br />

<ul>
  <li>When the User Switcher is triggered, a background overlay appears.</li>
  <li>User Switcher animates in from the top of the screen.</li>
  <li>Selecting an account will close the User Switcher.</li>
</ul>

<br />

### Scrolling

<br />

<ul>
  <li>
    If there are more than three accounts, the Accounts section will clip become a vertical scroll.
  </li>
  <li>The active account details (header) and sign out actions (footer) will remain sticky.</li>
</ul>
