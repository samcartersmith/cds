Once you’ve enabled CDS Libraries in your Figma file, you’ll have access to CDS resources for your design work. There are a variety of ways you can use CDS Libraries. Follow this guide to learn more about each option.

### Asset Panel

On the left sidebar, you'll see a tab named Asset Panel. Asset Panel is a visual interface for you to search and drag & drop components into your interface.

Asset Panel will show you all CDS Libraries you’ve activated. Keep in mind that it will also show you components from other libraries and components within the same file.

![CDS Libraries in Figma](/img/designers-guide/asset_library.png)

**Pro-tip:** You can use can access Asset Hub by shortcut:

- macOS: `⌥ + 2`
- Windows: `Alt + 2`

### Instances

The other way you can find and reuse components is by finding their instances and copying those. If they're attached, their copies will also remain attached.

#### Drag to copy

You may use drag to copy by also holding the modifier key. When you release the component from dragging, you'll have copied the component and created another instance of it.

Drag and hold the modifier key:

- macOS: `⌥`
- Windows: `Alt`

#### Copy and paste

You may also use the more traditional copy and paste technique.

Copy & Paste

- macOS: `⌘ + C` and then `⌘ + P`
- Windows: `Control + C` and then `Control + P`

#### Changing variants

From an instance, you may also change an instance to another instance. This can be a time saver if you're changing a button style or swapping icons.

Changing variants

- Right click on instance
- Hover over the **Change variant** option
- Select the variant you want to swap to

![CDS Libraries in Figma](/img/designers-guide/change_variant.png)

### Styles

In the right sidebar, you'll have the option to apply styles.

#### Text Styles

When selecting a text element, click on the four dot menu to see available text styles.

**Note**: Read our section on [Typography](/foundation/typography) for general guidance on our available text styles.

![CDS Libraries in Figma](/img/designers-guide/text_styles.png)

#### Color Styles

When selecting any type of element, you'll have the ability to set a fill and border style. These are accessed by clicking the four dot menu.

**Note**: Read our section on [Color](/foundation/color) for general guidance on our available color styles.

![CDS Libraries in Figma](/img/designers-guide/color_styles.png)

#### Shadow Styles

When selecting shadows, you'll have the ability to set a shadow style. These are accessed by clicking the four dot menu.

![CDS Libraries in Figma](/img/designers-guide/elevation_styles.png)
