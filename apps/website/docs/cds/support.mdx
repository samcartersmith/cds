We try our best to offer as many ways to support you.

** ⛔️ Please refrain from sending direct messages to members of CDS.** – This is often the slowest way to get a response to your question, and it can be distracting for CDS designers and engineers.

## Getting Help

1. Check documentation site (this place) - take a look at the primitive or component's page
2. If it's quick question, ask in [#ask-cds](http://go/ask-cds)
3. If it's not a quick question, chat with us in Office Hours [Request Office Hours](http://go/cds-help)

## Slack Channels

We have a few Slack Channels. We'd love you to join these:

### [#ask-cds](http://go/ask-cds)

**Why:** Support channel for all CDS work (product design, visual, illustration, and motion systems)

**Who:** Anyone at Coinbase

### [#announcements-cds](http://go/announcements-cds)

**Why:** Share program and component updates

**Who:** Anyone at Coinbase (only cds team can post, anyone can comment)

### [#cds-alerts](http://go/cds-alerts)

**Why:** If visual change was merged to master, it should ping cds-eng-adopters handle

**Who:** Product engineers who are implementing CDS

## Office Hours

**Every Wednesday and Thursday from 2-3pm PT.**

1. Find an open appointment slot via [go/cds-help](http://go/cds-help)
2. Add your topic and specify which CDS team members should be present
3. Hit save

> \*Meeting notes and relevant action items will be captured
