## Principles

### Anchored

Anchor the user while they are using Coinbase by showing them which account they are logged into at every point in their experience.

### Seamless

Harmoniously support the navigation between multiple account types, product experiences, and product-specific use cases.

### Connected

Allow for a cohesive Coinbase experience, where users can shift between their various business and personal accounts in one, unified place.

## Behavior

- Opening and closing

  - When the User Switcher is triggered, a background overlay appears.
  - User Switcher animates in from the top of the screen.
  - Selecting an account will close the User Switcher.

- Scrolling
  - If there are more than three accounts, the Accounts section will clip become a vertical scroll.
  - The active account details (header) and sign out actions (footer) will remain sticky.
