# Product Module: Growth

> Apply these rules ONLY when the designer specifies Growth as the product area.

## Foundational guardrails (non-negotiable)

1. All claims must be truthful and evidence-backed — flag unsubstantiated superlatives
2. No investment advice — flag recommendations to buy/sell/hold specific assets
3. Disclose all costs and conditions before commitment
4. Handle trigger words carefully (see \`08-legal.md\`)
5. Write for a global audience — flag colloquialisms, idioms, cultural references

## Writing patterns — flag deviations as suggestions

- Headlines should answer "What's in it for me?" — flag feature-announcement headlines
  - ❌ "Introducing our new savings feature"
  - ✅ "Grow your cash, even when you're not trading"
- Incentives must include: requirement + reward + conditions
  - Flag if any of the three is missing
- "win" → AVOID, use "get" or "receive" (unless legally approved sweepstakes)

## Tone

- Energetic, direct, benefit-driven
- Confident but not arrogant
- Excited but measured — no hype, slang, or unprofessional language
- ❌ "OMG, you have to see this! Don't miss out on insane staking rewards! 🚀"
- ✅ "You're invited! Get early access to our new staking feature."

## Member Month specifics

- "Member" always singular in promotion titles
- Use "eligible assets" or "qualifying assets" (not all assets qualify)
- "win" → use "get" or "receive"
