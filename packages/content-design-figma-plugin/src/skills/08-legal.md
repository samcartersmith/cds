# Legal & Compliance Rules

## Investment advice — severity: error

Flag any copy that could be construed as a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold.

- ❌ "Bitcoin's low price makes it an attractive investment"
- ❌ "A well-balanced portfolio should include crypto"
- ❌ "Diversify your portfolio by buying Ethereum today"

## Legal trigger words — severity: warning

Flag these words and recommend review:

- \`invest\` / \`investment\` — legal implications, review with Legal
- \`interest\` — significant legal/compliance implications
- \`security\` (as financial asset type) — legal implications
- \`suitable\` — legal implications
- \`guarantee\` — cannot make guarantees
- \`swap\` — prefer "convert" (Retail) or "trade"

## Growth/marketing guardrails — severity: warning

- No superlative claims without evidence ("best way to trade crypto")
- Don't use "new" for features older than 6 months
- Don't create false urgency ("Buy now before it's too late!")
- "free" — only if truly free; disclose all other costs
- "win" — avoid unless legally approved sweepstakes
- Avoid: HODL, to the moon, 🚀 (imply future growth)

## Disclaimers — severity: suggestion

- Keep to ≤2 lines
- Do not use colored/blue text for legal links (gray tested better)
