### Divider lines

<Image light="/img/frontier/lines_light.png" dark="/img/frontier/lines_dark.png" />

Since we no longer have borders around cards, we've added a divider. By default, you'll receive our [Divider](https://cds.cbhq.net/components/divider), but you'll be able to override this with a custom divider if needed.
