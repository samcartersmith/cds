Our design principles are a shared lens for how we evaluate design at Coinbase.

The principles help us make design decisions, uphold quality, ensure consistency at scale, and introduce new designers to how we work.

## Make crypto simple

Coinbase is the first step into crypto for millions of people. No matter what product we design, we should make it as simple as possible for them, while still offering the features they want.

🎭 Ask in critique:

- Can we remove this step?
- Can we automate this?
- Can we let the customer do this without leaving Coinbase?
- Can we test this to make sure it’s intuitive?

## Make crypto safe

Crypto can be a scary place for beginners and pros alike. We should do everything we can to build a product that helps customers avoid mistakes and feel that they’re safe using Coinbase.

🎭 Ask in critique:

- What could go wrong? How can we prevent it?
- Can we make this transaction feel safer?
- How can we help customers feel more confident using crypto?

## Make crypto bold

Coinbase should feel alive and fun to use. Charts should be fluid and engaging, with live data. Color, motion, and illustration should invite people in. Tasks that might be tedious in traditional finance should feel delightful.

🎭 Ask in critique:

- How can we use color and illustration here?
- Would motion, haptics, or sound improve this interaction?
- Does this copy engage customers?
- Can we add exclusive or insightful data?
- Is this a moment we should celebrate?

## Make crypto for everyone

Our products should welcome every target customer, no matter who they are, what language they speak, or where they live. We should guide them, anticipate what they want to do, and intelligently personalize their experience.

🎭 Ask in critique

- Can we add tips, guidance, or education?
- Can we make this more inclusive and accessible?
- How will this look localized?
- Can we anticipate what the customer wants to do next?
- Can we let the customer customize this interface?
