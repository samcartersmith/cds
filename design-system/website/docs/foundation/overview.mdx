CDS is an end-to-end design and development system for applying consistent visual styles
across react projects and products at Coinbase.

Components within CDS are powered by a `ThemeProvider` with a configurable scale, spectrum and palette. We have 7 total scales ranging from Extra Small (XS) to Extra-Extra-Extra Large (XXXL). A product can offer all scales to users, but we only recommend designing and developing products with two scales in mind Normal (Large) and Dense (Extra Small). Spectrum is an extensive color library with 8 color sets that has both light and dark mode versions. Palettes aim to align common color usages across interfaces. All of the colors used in our palettes come from spectrum colors.
