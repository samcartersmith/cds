We try our best to offer as many ways to support you.

** ⛔️ Please refrain from sending direct messages to members of CDS.** – This is often the slowest way to get a response to your question, and it can be distracting for CDS designers and engineers.

## Getting Help

1. Check documentation site (this place) - take a look at the primitive or component's page
2. If it's quick question, ask in [#ask-cds](http://go/ask-cds)
3. If it's not a quick question, chat with us in Office Hours [Request Office Hours](http://go/cds-help)

## Slack Channels

We have a few Slack Channels. We'd love you to join these:

- [#ask-cds](http://go/ask-cds) - This is our support channel on Slack, and it's a great second step in letting us know what's going on. Check this doc site first!
- [#coinbase-design-system](http://go/coinbase-design-system) - This is primary announcements channel, and is a great place to follow for updates.
- [#cds-working](http://go/cds-working) - This is our working channel where we'll show work and ask questions.

## Status

- [CDS Tracker](http://go/cds-tracker) - Take a look at our timelines
- **CDS Update** - Every Friday we post in [#coinbase-design-system](http://go/coinbase-design-system) with our weekly status for in-flight projects.
- **CDS Dispatch** - Every end of the month, we send out a newsletter with all work from the team that shipped that month.

## Office Hours

Every Wednesday and Thursday from 2:00-3:00 PM PT. Join [hangout](https://meet.google.com/otu-fska-ywo).

Please fill out the [sign-up sheet](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeH1aUTyegkrycxq3PaufQzD8I6OOarlJfeni2z-MOLFxY4-A/viewform) the day before if you want to make sure particular members of the CDS team will be present. It will also give us more time to prepare for your questions.
