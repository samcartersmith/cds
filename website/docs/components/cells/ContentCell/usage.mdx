A cell row is divided into the following 3 columns:

- Media: A type of [media](./cell-media), like icon or avatar.
- Content: A title, subtitle, or description. A special "meta" field can be displayed at the top right.
- Accessory: A whitelisted icon displayed at the end.

```jsx live
<ContentCell
  title="A deep dive into Eth 2.0, scaling that lets users buy the entire crypto market"
  subtitle="The Block - Ethereum 2"
  description="On this episode of The Scoop, Ethereum 2.0 developer and Prysmatic Labs founder Preston Van Loon and Joe Sticco broke down Cryptex. The post A deep dive into Eth 2.0, scaling and a project that lets users buy the entire crypto market appeared first on The Block."
  meta="March 2nd, 2021"
  media={
    <CellMedia
      type="image"
      title="Eth 2.0"
      source="https://dynamic-assets.coinbase.com/2954170d91149bea19e2d2eab8acc2f50ff4446b4b6fb09a7983ad7a481c636e6b29c5e09cf90f49f57dcef30ef7ff50bb99ad4fc068cf43265ad135c590fa7d/news_article_images/28216c10d33e3f2147fe05aa8a27bf4b9620dd658ce0be6c482b5629176e52e4.png"
    />
  }
  onPress={console.log}
/>
```

### Vertical alignment

Cells are designed with built-in padding on _all_ edges, and because of this, _must_ expand the full-width of their parent container. Any non-cell sibling content should vertically align their left-side content with the left-side content of the cell (title, description, media), not including the padding.

#### Correct

An example of this implemented **correctly** is demonstrated below. Notice that all text is vertically aligned. This is achieved with a start spacing of 3 on the title!

```jsx live
<>
  <TextTitle3 as="p" spacingStart={3} spacingBottom={1}>
    Section title
  </TextTitle3>

  <ContentCell
    title="Something very cool here"
    description="Lorem ipsum dolor sit a met. Lorem ipsum dolor sit a met."
    onPress={console.log}
    selected
  />
  <ContentCell
    title="Something very cool here"
    description="Lorem ipsum dolor sit a met. Lorem ipsum dolor sit a met."
    onPress={console.log}
  />
  <ContentCell
    title="Something very cool here"
    description="Lorem ipsum dolor sit a met. Lorem ipsum dolor sit a met."
    onPress={console.log}
  />
</>
```

#### Incorrect

An example of this implemented **incorrectly** is demonstrated below. Notice how the title does not align vertically with the text in the cell.
