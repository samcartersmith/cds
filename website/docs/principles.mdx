Our design principles are a shared lens for how we evaluate design at Coinbase.

The principles help us make design decisions, uphold quality, ensure consistency at scale, and introduce new designers to how we work.

## Make crypto simple

Coinbase is famous for being the first step into crypto for millions of people. No matter what product we design, we should make it as simple as possible, while still offering the features our customers want.

🎭 Ask in critique:

- Can we remove this step?
- Can we automate this?
- Can we let the customer do this without leaving Coinbase?
- Can we test this to make sure it’s intuitive?

## Make crypto safe

Crypto can be a scary place for beginners and pros alike. We should do everything we can to build a product that helps customers avoid mistakes and feel they’re safe with Coinbase.

🎭 Ask in critique:

- What could go wrong? How can we prevent it?
- Can we make this transaction feel safer?
- Can we give the customer more confidence?

## Make crypto bold

Coinbase should feel alive and fun to use. Live data should be just that: live. Tasks that might be tedious in traditional finance should feel delightful. Charts should be fluid and engaging.

🎭 Ask in critique:

- How can we use color and illustration here?
- Would haptics or animation improve this interaction?
- Does this copy engage customers?
- Can we add exclusive or insightful data?
- Is this a moment we should celebrate?

## Make crypto for everyone

Our products should feel welcoming to every target customer, no matter who they are, what language they speak, or where they live. Coinbase should guide you, anticipate what you want to do, and intelligently personalize your data.

🎭 Ask in critique

- Can we add tips, guidance, or education?
- Can we make this more inclusive and accessible?
- How will this look localized?
- Can we anticipate what the customer wants to do next?
- Can we let the customer customize this interface?
