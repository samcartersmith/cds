We have 7 total scales ranging from Extra Small (XS) to Extra-Extra-Extra Large (XXXL). Your product can offer all scales to users, but we only recommend designing and developing products with two scales in mind Normal (Large) and Dense (Extra Small).

## Dense

Dense scale is our extra small scale. It’s an ideal scale for web tools that need to accommodate a lot of data.

## Normal

Normal scale is our large scale. It’s an ideal scale for mobile products or landing pages.

## Layout considerations

Dynamic type can have a significant impact on layout, especially if scaled up. Consider different solutions to maintain original layout intent, and ensure product quality.
