## Secondary is now gray

<Image light="/img/frontier/secondary_light.png" dark="/img/frontier/secondary_dark.png" />

In line with the Unified App concept, we’ve shifted the style of our Secondary button, and now it’s gray. We’ve also tweaked our Secondary button to no longer have a border.

## Updated Yellow

<Image light="/img/frontier/yellow_light.png" dark="/img/frontier/yellow_dark.png" />

Our brand uses a more vibrant yellow, so we’ve modified our yellow ramp to support brand's needs.

<Image light="/img/frontier/teal_indigo_light.png" dark="/img/frontier/teal_indigo_dark.png" />

We’re excited to share that we’ve added Teal and fully exposed Indigo. Those colors will be available immediately without needing a FeatureFlagProvider.
