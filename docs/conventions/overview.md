## Terminology

**CDS** - Coinbase Design System
**UI Systems** - the joint team of USE and USD see [UI Systems Charter 2023](https://docs.google.com/document/u/0/d/1jruW1iHw3Q2Yhgts1Sklegrd1_CZcAevYr0C_Bqd6AY/edit)
**UI Systems Engineering (USE)** - the engineering team that owns CDS
**UI Systems Design (USD)** - the design counterpart to USE that owns CDS
**KTLO** - Keeping the lights on
**PE** - Product Engineer
**PED** - Product, Engineering, Design
**PD** - Product Designer
**Breaking Change** - The contribution will result in a change to a component or aspect of the design system’s API, parameters, visual treatment, or component composition that is not backwards compatible.
